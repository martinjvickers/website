Xiaoqi Feng's Lab

Xiaoqi Feng is a plant geneticist at the John Innes Centre, Norwich, UK. Her lab studies the development and epigenetics of plant germlines.

Meet the lab!

Male germline-specific DNA methylation

We discovered specific de novo methylation of genic loci in the male germline which is necessary for successful Arabidopsis meiosis.

More Info

Histone H1 depletion in pollen vegetative cells

We characterised the extent of TE expression in Arabidopsis pollen vegetative cells, and determined that their expression relies on loss of histone H1 in concert with the DEMETER demethylase.

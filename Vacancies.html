Join Us

We are always open to informal enquiries from highly motivated postdocs/students with an interest in;

  • chromatin biology/genomics
  • developmental biology
  • genetics
  • cell biology
  • structural biology/biochemistry

Interested candidates should contact Xiaoqi Feng directly by e-mail (xiaoqi.feng [at] jic.ac.uk), including a CV, and a short statement.

If you are interested in a position as a PhD student, please also have a look at the website of the JIC for Postgraduate Research to learn about their graduate programme.
